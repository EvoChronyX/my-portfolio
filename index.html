Qiskit Advocate

IBM Quantum - Qiskit Advocate

I am a proud Qiskit Advocate, a recognition awarded by IBM Quantum to passionate enthusiasts who actively contribute to the global quantum computing community. This program connects a network of developers, researchers, and educators dedicated to advancing the Qiskit open-source framework and sharing knowledge with others. As an Advocate, I engage with a network of experts, contribute to cutting-edge projects, and help educate the next generation of quantum developers.

My journey to becoming an Advocate was solidified by earning the Quantum Excellence (Advanced) badge. This achievement is awarded to participants who demonstrate a deep understanding of quantum concepts and practical Qiskit skills, typically by successfully completing the advanced graded lab activities during the Qiskit Global Summer School. Earning this badge was a key prerequisite that demonstrated my commitment and technical proficiency, paving the way for my acceptance into the Advocate program.
